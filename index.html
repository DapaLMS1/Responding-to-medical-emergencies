1. Ensure Safety ►

Prioritize assessing **danger to yourself, other staff, and remaining patients**, then the patient in distress. Remove any immediate hazards to prevent further injury.

2. Assess Life Risk ►

Quickly check the patient's **responsiveness, airway, and breathing** (DRSABCD protocol: Danger, Response, Send for Help, Airway, Breathing, CPR, Defibrillation).

3. Call for Help ►

Immediately **dial 000 for an ambulance** and alert the dentist/senior staff. Clearly state the emergency, location, and patient's condition.

4. Provide Basic Aid ►

While waiting, perform **basic life support or first aid** within your training, such as opening an airway, starting CPR if needed, or assisting with emergency equipment as directed.

5. Monitor & Support ►

Continuously **monitor the patient** and provide information to arriving paramedics. Your role is crucial in recognizing the emergency, ensuring safety, activating professional help, and assisting the team within your scope of practice.
